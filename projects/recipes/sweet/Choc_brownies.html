Chocolate Blackbean Brownies

Easy and tasty, relatively healthy chocolate blackbean brownies - you can hardly taste the beans!]

Ingredients

  • 400g can black beans
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 bananas (200g)
  • 3 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 15g Cocoa Powder
  • 60g protein powder (2 scoops)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Line a 25cm x 15cm tin with parchment paper.
  2. Add beans and banana to a bowl and blitz with stick blender.
  3. Add all ingredients into a bowl and mix well with a fork or spoon.
  4. Pour ingredients into lined tin and place in preheated oven.
  5. Bake at 180° for 15 minutes.\footnote{A skewer should come out with a bit of goop on it but not too much liquid.}
  6. Rest for 15 minutes to cool in tin, then place on wire rack (parchment underneath) and cool for at least 45 minutes.

Prep time: 15 min · Cook time: 15 min · Serves: 9
